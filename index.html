About Me

I first started my coding journey halfway through 2019. At the time I was pursuing dreams of music, but was also in a transformative time in my life. I decided to give website development a shot because I knew I wanted something creative but also challenging. I started doing 1 hour a day of coding in August 2019 and I enjoyed it. It wasn't until I first started at Nashville Software School where that enjoyment became genuine interest and passion.

Nashville Software School has made such a profound impact on my life. The amount I learned in 6 months is truly astonishing. I have learned to code so many amazing websites, and have improved my already strong skills as a leader, but also increased my skill level as a follower. I enjoy the challenge of class, but am eager to see the challenges that lie ahead.
